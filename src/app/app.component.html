Techniques

In the frame of trading robot project, we developed a prediction algorithm for 8 steps ahead. The core of the algorithm is a convolutional neural network (CNN), which is trained on pre-prepared images. Each image (see Fig.) is a ROI of a time-frequency wavelet spectrogram with a size of 32x24. At the classifier's output we get the probability of the presence of a cyclic channel pattern for the future 8 steps. The algorithm performs scanning of 8 frequency channels in real time simultaneously.
